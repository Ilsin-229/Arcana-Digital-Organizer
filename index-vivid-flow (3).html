Features

Five tools for staying organized

Search

search_vault

Sweeps the whole vault to find the note you need.

Write & update

create_or_update_note

Creates notes or overwrites them in place.

Move

move_note

Relocates notes to fit your PARA structure.

Restructure

reorganize_note

Rewrites messy notes into a clean format.

Daily digest

synthesize_daily_file

Merges a day's notes into one automatic summary.
